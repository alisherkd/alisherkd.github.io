First Training Class

In the beginning, stick to a gentle regime, coming to the gym - two workouts a week will be quite enough. The body must adapt, get used to the loads, and the muscles "get over it". Practice for 30-40 minutes to get used to the regime and stretch. Then you can switch to training 3-4 times a week for 60-70 minutes.

Second Training Class

The most important thing in the hall is to monitor the equipment. Do not train with dumbbells and do not bend over the disc with a slouched back – all this can lead to injuries. Focus on doing the exercises. Feel the muscle group you are working with by sending a nerve impulse.

Third Training Class

For beginners, two or three approaches for each exercise will be enough. When you have adapted, you can do four sets in the gym. For girls, this is the best option. The main thing is that you feel the muscles while doing exercises. That is, it should be really hard on the last reps, the only way classes in the gym will be effective.
